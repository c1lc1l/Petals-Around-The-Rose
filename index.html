The name of the game is Petals Around the Rose. The computer will roll five dice and ask you to guess the score for the roll.

The score will always be zero or an even number. Your mission is to work out how the computer calculates the score and become a Pentate of the Rose.

How to Play:

  1. Click on "Roll Dice" to start the game.
  2. Type in your guess for the score.
  3. Click on "Accept Guess" to get the result.
  4. Then either click on "Roll Dice" for another turn.
